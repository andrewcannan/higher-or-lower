Higher or Lower

Are you feeling lucky, Punk?!

The rules are simple. A card will be drawn face down, your goal is to guess the value of the card between Ace and King. You will recieve three attempts to correctly guess the card, on each failed attempt you will recieve a hint to wether the card is higher or lower than your guess. You have 99 seconds per turn, Good Luck!
